Les Cadors
Monthly tournament · By invitation · Starting June 1st, 2026
Password protected

Each month, the top 20 players of the Trio Quotidien from the previous month receive a personal invitation with the tournament password. No relegation, no subscription — finishing in the top 20 of the Trio is all it takes to come back the following month.

The top 20 Trio players of month M receive an invitation on the 1st of month M+1
The top 5 Petits Cadors players (who also play the Trio) complete the list — up to 25 players in total
4 deals per day, joker management, monthly standings reset to zero
Invitations are sent by email at 6 a.m. on the 1st of each month

Les Petits Cadors
Monthly tournament · Open to all · Starting June 1st, 2026
Open access · No password

A tournament with no barriers, open to everyone. It offers an extra chance to qualify for Les Cadors the following month — especially after a tough month in the Trio.

No registration required, no password
The top 5 players of Les Petits Cadors who also play the Trio are invited to Les Cadors the following month
Players outside the Trio are welcome — but only regular Trio players can qualify for Les Cadors
4 deals per day, monthly standings with jokers
💡 Your best chance to recover: had a rough month in the Trio? Les Petits Cadors stays open until the last day of the month.

Le Trio Quotidien
Daily tournament · Open to all · All year round
Open access · No password

The daily meeting place for all players. 3 deals per day in IMP, playable at your own pace throughout the day. Consistency pays off — this is the tournament that determines most of the invitations to Les Cadors.

3 deals per day in IMP format, playable at any time of day
3-joker system that cancels your worst scores of the month, unlocked progressively:
• 1st joker from 9 days played
• 2nd joker from 18 days played
• 3rd joker from 27 days played
🔥 Consistency bonus — the longest consecutive streak of days with a positive score is converted into bonus points and added to the monthly total. Absences are neutral, a negative score resets the counter to zero (in effect from July 2026)
Monthly ranking reset on the 1st of each month · Annual cumulative calculated in parallel
The monthly top 30 is automatically invited to the Les Cadors tournament the following month
✉️ To receive your Cadors invitation if you are in the top 30, remember to share your email address via the « Share my email » button at the top of this page — without an email I cannot send you the tournament password.

Le SergeLive
Daily tournament · Open to all · All year round
Open access · No password
✦ This tournament is a tribute to our friend Serge, who left us too soon. It follows the format of his LEROY-S4 tournament that many of us knew and loved — his way of bringing online bridge to life every day lives on through SergeLive.

A fuller format than the Trio: 7 deals per day, with a key rule: only positive scores count. A bad day doesn't penalise you — it simply scores 0. This mechanic rewards consistency without punishing tough days.

7 deals per day — a longer format than a standard tournament
Only positive scores are counted — a negative day counts as 0 (no jokers needed, no score to make up)
⭐ The 15th of each month counts double — the 15th is the date Serge left us to join the stars. That day, the score achieved is counted double in his memory (in effect from July 2026 — Thursday scores are no longer doubled)
🔥 Consistency bonus — at the end of each month, the longest streak of consecutive days with a positive score is converted into bonus points and added to the total. Playing every day at your best is rewarded (in effect from July 2026)
Monthly ranking reset on the 1st of each month · Annual cumulative calculated in parallel
A cross-qualification route exists to Les Cadors: finish in both the top 20 SergeLive and top 50 Trio for the month
✉️ To receive your Cadors invitation if you meet the double condition (top 20 SergeLive + top 50 Trio for the month), remember to share your email address via the « Share my email » button at the top of this page — without an email I cannot send you the tournament password.

Les Cadors
Monthly tournament · By invitation · Starting June 1st, 2026
Password protected

Each month, the top players receive a personal invitation by email with the tournament password. Invitations come from 4 qualification routes that reward consistency (Trio), versatility (SergeLive × Trio), second chances (Petits Cadors) and defending merit (Cadors podium from the previous month). No favouritism — just clear rules.

✉️ Important — to receive this invitation, you must have shared your email address with me. If you haven't done so yet, click « Share my email » at the top of this page. Without an email I cannot send you the tournament password.
Top 30 of the Trio Quotidien from the previous month
Top 5 of the Petits Cadors (who also play the Trio) — a recovery route open to all
Top 20 SergeLive ∩ Top 50 Trio — rewards players active on both fronts
Cadors podium (Top 3) from the previous month — to defend their title, from the 2nd tournament
4 deals per day · Jokers · Monthly ranking reset · Invitation by email on the 1st of each month at 6am
💡 On average 38 to 42 guests per month. Provisional list updated daily: Cadors candidates for the month.

Les Petits Cadors
Monthly tournament · Open to all · Starting June 1st, 2026
Open access · No password

A tournament with no barriers, open to everyone. It offers an extra chance to join Les Cadors the following month — especially after a tough month in the Trio.

No prior registration, no password
The top 5 players of the Petits Cadors who also play the Trio are invited to Les Cadors the following month
Players outside the Trio are welcome — but only regular Trio players can qualify for Les Cadors
4 deals per day, monthly ranking with jokers (thresholds: 9/18/27 days from July 2026)
🔥 Consistency bonus — the longest consecutive streak of days with a positive score is converted into bonus points and added to the monthly total. Absences are neutral, a negative score resets the counter to zero (in effect from July 2026)
💡 Your best recovery route: had a tough month in the Trio? Les Petits Cadors stays open until the last day of the month.
✉️ To receive your Cadors invitation if you are in the top 5 (and playing the Trio), remember to share your email address via the « Share my email » button at the top of this page — without an email I cannot send you the tournament password.

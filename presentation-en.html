Les Cadors
Monthly tournament · By invitation · Starting June 1st, 2026
Password protected

Each month, the strongest players of the ecosystem receive a personal invitation with the tournament password. Invitations come from 4 qualifying paths that reward consistency (Trio), versatility (SergeLive crossed with Trio), a second chance (Petits Cadors) and the defending champions' privilege (Cadors podium from the previous month). No favouritism — just clear rules.

Top 20 of the Trio Quotidien for the previous month
Top 5 of the Petits Cadors (also playing the Trio) — recovery path open to everyone
Top 20 of SergeLive ∩ Top 50 of the Trio — rewards players active on both fronts
Cadors podium (Top 3) from the previous month — title defenders, starting with the 2nd tournament
4 deals per day · Jokers · Monthly standings reset to zero · Email invitation on the 1st of the month at 6 a.m.
💡 Around 28 to 32 invited players each month. Live preview list updated daily: Cadors candidates of the month.

Les Petits Cadors
Monthly tournament · Open to all · Starting June 1st, 2026
Open access · No password

A tournament with no barriers, open to everyone. It offers an extra chance to qualify for Les Cadors the following month — especially after a tough month in the Trio.

No registration required, no password
The top 5 players of Les Petits Cadors who also play the Trio are invited to Les Cadors the following month
Players outside the Trio are welcome — but only regular Trio players can qualify for Les Cadors
4 deals per day, monthly standings with jokers
💡 Your best chance to recover: had a rough month in the Trio? Les Petits Cadors stays open until the last day of the month.
